About Syndal Bakery

Located at 242 Blackburn Rd, Glen Waverley, Syndal Bakery has been serving freshly baked breads, delicious pastries, and your favourite coffee since day one. We pride ourselves on quality, local flavours, and community spirit.

Our friendly team wakes up early every day (except Sundays) to bake fresh products you can trust and love.
